About 11-Health

Independent, physician-led second-opinion and preventive-care coordination—helping patients worldwide know more, live better, and extend their healthspan.

Why We Built 11-Health

At 11-Health, we believe better health starts with better information.

As demand grows for preventive care, early detection, and diagnostic clarity, we identified two key barriers:

  • The high cost of established programs at traditional institutions
  • The complexity and time involved in coordinating fragmented services across multiple providers

We set out to change that.

What if you could access reliable, clinically rigorous diagnostics—all in one place, customized to your needs and budget, with centralized results and coordination with your physicians back home?

11-Health exists to make world-class diagnostic and wellness services more accessible, personalized, and empowering—without compromising on quality. From comprehensive labs to advanced imaging and genetic analysis, we’re here to support your entire health journey.
